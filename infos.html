Download Builds From Google Drive

GD Tutorial Bypass By @Jhonatan

Step 1

  • Open your Google Drive and create a folder

    • Step 2

  • On your Windows, create a .txt file with any name
  • Place the .txt file in your created folder on Google Drive

    • Step 3

  • Go to the build you want to download
  • Click on the file option
  • Click on create a shortcut and choose your folder

    • Step 4

  • Inside your folder, hold down Ctrl and click on the .txt file and the build
  • Then click the download button
  • Wait a while, and you've successfully downloaded the build.
